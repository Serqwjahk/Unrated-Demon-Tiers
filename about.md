# Unrated Demon Tiers

Pick the demon tier of your own unrated demon levels and share it with everyone using the mod (if it gets indexed).

Unrated demons all look the same (insane) until they get an official rate. This mod makes them REAL demons and it lets you choose which tier it shows: Easy, Medium, Hard, Insane or Extreme.

## Usage

1. Open the info page of one of your own unrated demon levels.
2. Press **T** (configurable in settings) to open the tier picker.
3. Pick a tier.
4. The **Remove** button restores the voted difficulty and stops sharing.

## Notes

- Viewing tiers needs no account or setup.
- Sharing your own uses [Argon](https://github.com/GlobedGD/argon), which proves the account is yours without your password ever leaving the game.
- The server also checks that the level belongs to you, is unrated and was uploaded as a demon, so nobody can set tiers on levels they don't own.
